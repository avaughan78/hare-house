Our Methodology ↓
The Process

The 14-Day Diagnostic

A surgical, fixed-timeline engagement designed to identify bottlenecks and de-risk your technical roadmap without slowing down your team.

1
Days 1 — 3

Discovery

Repository access, technical documentation audit, and initial data gathering from observability tools.

2
Days 4 — 8

Deep Analysis

Manual code review and stakeholder interviews to uncover "hidden" friction and architectural debt.

3
Days 9 — 12

Synthesis

Risk-rating findings and drafting the prioritized roadmap for the executive briefing.

4
Day 14

The Briefing

Delivery of findings and a 1:1 walkthrough of the remediation strategy.

Included Deliverables:
  • Executive Summary: A 1-page business-risk briefing.
  • Technical Risk Register: Prioritized list of debt and security gaps.
  • Remediation Roadmap: A 90-day execution plan for your team.
  • Velocity Metrics: Benchmarked efficiency scores.
